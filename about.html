Welcome to Pune Sports Club At Pune Sports Cub, we are more than just a sports club – we are a community bound by a shared love for sports/activity. Established in 2023, we have been a cornerstone in Shivaji nagar for 1 year, promoting a culture of sportsmanship, teamwork, and personal growth.

Our Mission >

Our mission is simple: to foster a positive and inclusive environment where individuals of all ages and skill levels can come together to enjoy and excel in sport/activity. We believe in the power of sports to inspire, unite, and transform lives.

GET INVOLVED.

Joining Pune Sports Club means becoming part of a dynamic and thriving sports community. Whether you're looking to compete at the highest level, improve your skills, or simply enjoy the camaraderie, there's a place for you here. Explore our website to learn more about our programs, events, and how you can get involved. We look forward to welcoming you to the PUNE SPORTS CLUB family!
